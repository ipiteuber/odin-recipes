Pizza of Bruno Bucciarati

Description

A versatile and beloved favorite, pizza features a crispy dough base topped with savory tomato sauce, melty mozzarella cheese, and an array of toppings such as pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, and olives. Perfect for casual dinners and parties.

Ingredients

  • 1 pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • 1/2 cup pizza sauce
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup sliced bell peppers
  • 1/4 cup sliced black olives
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 tsp dried oregano

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza stone or baking sheet.
  4. Spread the pizza sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  6. Arrange the pepperoni, mushrooms, bell peppers, and black olives on top of the cheese.
  7. Sprinkle the grated Parmesan cheese and dried oregano over the toppings.
  8. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  9. Let the pizza cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
