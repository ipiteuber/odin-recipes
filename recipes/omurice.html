Omurice of Josuke Higashikata

Description

A Japanese comfort food combining a flavorful fried rice mixture with a fluffy omelet. The rice is typically seasoned with ketchup and soy sauce, and the dish is often garnished with additional ketchup, making it a satisfying and kid-friendly meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1/2 onion, chopped
  • 1/2 cup mixed vegetables (carrots, peas, corn)
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken, diced
  • 2 tbsp ketchup
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tbsp milk
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat 1 tbsp of vegetable oil in a large pan over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped onion and cook until translucent.
  3. Add mixed vegetables and diced chicken, and cook for 3-4 minutes.
  4. Stir in cooked rice, ketchup, and soy sauce. Cook for another 2-3 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. In a bowl, beat the eggs with milk.
  6. Heat 1 tbsp of vegetable oil in a separate pan over medium heat.
  7. Pour half of the beaten eggs into the pan, spreading evenly.
  8. Cook until the eggs are almost set, then place half of the fried rice mixture in the center of the omelet.
  9. Fold the edges of the omelet over the rice, then carefully transfer to a plate.
  10. Repeat with the remaining eggs and rice mixture.
  11. Serve the omurice hot, optionally garnished with extra ketchup.
