Lasagna of Giorno Giovanna

Description

A classic Italian dish that layers wide flat pasta with a rich meat sauce, creamy ricotta cheese, and gooey mozzarella, all baked to perfection. This comforting and hearty meal is perfect for family gatherings and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (15 oz each) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tsp dried basil
  • 2 tsp dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 container (15 oz) ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, chopped onion, and minced garlic over medium heat until the beef is browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Stir in the tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, basil, oregano, salt, and pepper. Simmer for 20 minutes.
  5. In a bowl, mix the ricotta cheese with the egg until well combined.
  6. In a 9x13 inch baking dish, spread a thin layer of the meat sauce.
  7. Place 3 lasagna noodles on top of the sauce.
  8. Spread 1/3 of the ricotta mixture over the noodles.
  9. Sprinkle 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese over the ricotta.
  10. Repeat layers twice, ending with a final layer of meat sauce and Parmesan cheese.
  11. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  13. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving.
